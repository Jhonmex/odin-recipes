Japan recipes

sushi

Sushi is traditionally made with medium-grain white rice, though it can be prepared with brown rice or short-grain rice. It is very often prepared with seafood, such as squid, eel, yellowtail, salmon, tuna or imitation crab meat.

udon

Udon noodles are thick and have a chewy texture, providing a large bite. On the other hand, ramen noodles are thin and have a firm yet chewy texture, allowing them to hold up well in the flavorful broth of ramen soup.

onigiri

Onigiri (おにぎり) are Japanese rice balls made of steamed rice that you compress into a triangular, ball, or cylinder shape and are usually wrapped in a nori seaweed sheet.
